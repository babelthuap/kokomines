KokoMines

Multiplayer Minesweeper

  • Left click to reveal a tile.
  • Right click to place a flag.
  • Try to reveal all the safe tiles.
  • If you click on a mine, game over!
  • Uncovered safe tiles show the number of adjacent mines (up to 8).
  • Place flags where you think there are mines.
